FAQs

Common questions about getting started.

What happens after I submit this form?+
You'll receive a confirmation, and Dr. Amara's team will reach out within 1 business day to schedule your free 20-minute consultation call — by phone or video, whichever you prefer.
Is the consultation really free?+
Yes. The 20-minute consultation carries no cost and no obligation. It's simply a chance for us to talk briefly and decide together whether working together feels right.
Can I book directly for couples therapy?+
Yes — select "Couples Therapy" as the session type. Ideally both partners should be available for the initial consultation call, though it's not required.
Do you see clients outside Kenya?+
Yes, online sessions are available internationally via secure video call. Time zone differences are usually easy to work around — mention your location and preferred times in the form.
What if I'm not sure therapy is right for me yet?+
That's exactly what the free consultation is for. There's no pressure to commit to anything — it's simply a conversation to help you decide.
Is my information kept confidential?+
Yes. Information submitted through this form is used only to schedule and prepare for your consultation, and is handled with the same confidentiality as all clinical information.
