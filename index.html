Are You Feeling Overwhelmed,
Anxious, or Stuck?

You're not alone. Anxiety and stress are now the most common reasons people seek therapy — and many are reaching out for support earlier than ever before. If you've been carrying the weight of burnout, grief, relationship strain, or simply feeling disconnected from yourself, that's a signal worth listening to.

Therapy is a space to slow down, make sense of what you're carrying, and build the kind of inner steadiness that lasts beyond a single conversation. You don't have to have the right words to begin.

HI, I'M

Dr. Mary

CLINICAL PSYCHOLOGIST · SENTARI FOUNDER

My work sits at the intersection of evidence-based psychology and genuine human warmth. I help clients navigate anxiety, burnout, trauma, life transitions, and the quieter ache of feeling like something is off but not knowing what to call it.

I draw from cognitive-behavioural therapy, trauma-informed care, and somatic approaches because real change rarely lives in only one of those rooms. If you've been thinking about therapy but it feels like a big step — I get it. The first conversation is always the hardest, and I'll meet you there with patience and zero pressure.

More About My Approach

Individual Psychotherapy

Therapy is a collaborative process between you and a warm, experienced clinician — a confidential space to explore the patterns, emotions, and experiences shaping your life right now.

Through evidence-based techniques and honest conversation, we work together to ease distress, strengthen mental wellbeing, and help you reconnect with the parts of yourself that have felt distant or hard to access.
