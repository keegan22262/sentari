FAQs

Frequently asked questions.

How do I know if I need therapy?+
If something feels persistently heavy, confusing, or out of your control — that's enough. You don't need to be in crisis to deserve support. Many people find therapy most useful when life feels "fine but not right." Trust that instinct.
Is everything I share kept confidential?+
Yes. All sessions are strictly confidential under professional ethical guidelines and Kenyan law. The only exceptions are specific situations where there is risk of serious harm to yourself or others — and I will always discuss this with you openly if such a situation arises.
Do you offer online sessions?+
Yes — online sessions are available for clients anywhere in Kenya and internationally. Sessions are conducted via a secure, encrypted video platform. Many clients find online therapy just as effective as in-person, with the added comfort of being in your own space.
How long will I need to be in therapy?+
This varies widely depending on what you're working on. Some people find meaningful relief in 8–12 focused sessions. Others choose to work together for a year or more. We'll regularly review how things are going and you always decide when you're ready to conclude.
Do you work with insurance?+
I currently work as a private-pay practice. I can provide receipts and documentation that some insurers accept for reimbursement — please check your specific policy. If cost is a barrier, reach out directly to discuss sliding-scale options.
